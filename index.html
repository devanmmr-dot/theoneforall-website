The Problem

Running a fleet in South Africa
is unnecessarily complex.

Transport operators juggle spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, manual invoices, and disconnected compliance tools — losing time and money every day.

📊
No financial visibility
Revenue, CPK, and margins scattered across spreadsheets. No real-time view of profitability per contract or vehicle.
📋
Manual invoicing
Hours spent manually creating invoices after every trip. Errors, delays, and missed charges cost you thousands monthly.
⚠️
Compliance risk
HSEQ incidents, driver compliance, and HR/IR cases managed on paper — one audit away from serious liability.

Everything you need,
in one platform.

The One For All replaces your spreadsheets, manual processes, and disconnected tools with a single, intelligent platform built specifically for South African fleet operators.

✓
Live revenue tracking — trips auto-calculate revenue based on contract rates and fuel adjustments
✓
Automated invoicing — invoices generated directly from completed trips, branded with your company details
✓
Full HSEQ, HR/IR, and compliance management — digital, auditable, and always up to date
✓
Driver intelligence — league scoring, fatigue monitoring, and incident tracking per driver

What's included

Every module your fleet needs.

No add-ons. No surprises. Everything is included in your subscription.

🚚
Trip Management
Full order-to-delivery workflow. Dispatch board, turnaround times, OBC integration, and real-time trip status.
💰
Revenue Engine
Contract rates (per trip, km, CPL, per ton) with monthly fuel adjustments. Revenue auto-calculated per trip.
🧾
Automated Invoicing
Import completed trips directly into invoices. Branded PDFs with your logo, VAT, banking details — generated in seconds.
📈
P&L Reports
Flash-style P&L with Actual, Forecast, Budget, and Variance columns. Monthly budget setup per tenant.
⛽
Fuel Management
Full fuel transaction history, CPL tracking, monthly adjustments, and anomaly detection.
🏆
Driver Intelligence
Driver league scoring — Elite, Safe, Monitor, High Risk. OBC events, compliance, fuel, on-time, and fatigue scores.
⚠️
HSEQ & Incidents
Incident reporting, CAPA management, risk register, TBT tracker, SHE committee, and LTIFR dashboard.
👥
HR / IR Module
Employee records, disciplinary cases, hearings, appeals, and IR case management — all digital and auditable.
🔧
Maintenance & Fleet
Vehicle health scores, maintenance scheduling, tyre management, and full service history per vehicle.
